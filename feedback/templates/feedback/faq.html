Frequently Asked Questions

Users can report issues through the 'Report' section of our website. Fill out the form provided and submit it. All reports are confidential and will be processed by our dedicated team promptly.

We take your privacy seriously. All information provided is stored securely and only accessible to authorized personnel. We do not share your information with third parties without your explicit consent.

After submitting a report, it will be reviewed by our team. You may be contacted for further information if needed. All efforts are made to investigate reports thoroughly and take appropriate actions based on findings.
